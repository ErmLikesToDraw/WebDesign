#Site Plan

##Home Page 

**Goal** To indruduce the band, what we do, and provide a short gallery to give a visual

###Structure
**Navigation** Home, Info and History, dropdown menu with News and Updates, Sign Up, and Blog Posts. On the left side with the icon on the left.
**Header** Folk style image
**Quote** Lessons in Folk, to spark interest
**Hero** Roots 2026 Blog Page
**Main Content** Intro paragraph, Image, short gallery of Roots photographs
**Call to Action** buttons with links to all other pages of the site(matching the nav bar)
**Footer** Copyright and socials
