Yellowjackets follows the titled girls soccer team as they navigate the wilderness after a plane crash. The story uses every character for a differnt arc and purpose, nicely tying the tale of survival with the aftermath of trauma several years later. A combination of mystery and thrill truly make this show worth a watch

Episode 1

The team is introduced and 25 years later some of the members recount a traumatic period of time before they arrived at nationals

Episode 2

After the plane touches down in the woods, the first few days of aftermath are lived. In the future, new relationships begin with the surviving members

Episode 3

Three days after the crash, the team discusses their survival options while in the future some older members visit an old friend
